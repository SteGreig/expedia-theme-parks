Opening Hours

  • Monday to Friday: 12pm – 10pm
  • Saturday to Sunday: 11am – 10pm

The Berjaya Times Square was named as the biggest indoor theme park for all ages in Malaysia. This park offers impressive number of attractions, from entertainment stages, shopping, cafes, to kids’ playground areas and theatres.

Berjaya Times Square Theme Park has separate ride areas for guests of different ages. The Fantasy Garden on Level 7 is for children aged only from 4 to 12 years of age. This level offers more kid-friendly and mellow rides such as Crazy Bus, Botanic Drive and a Carousel Buddy-Go-Round. This theme park has also alternative, seasonal rides with future-forward technologies, such as the 5D Simulator and Futurift VR Oculus.

In Galaxy Station on level 5, you will find thrilling 3D and roller coaster rides aimed for children after 13 years of age and above. On this level, you will find the record-breaking for longest indoor roller coaster in Asia ride: The Supersonic Odyssey.

The Go Genting Express Bus will take you directly to the theme park. The bus service operates from different stations in Kuala Lumpur: Putra LRT Gombak Station, KL Sentral and Pudu Sentral.

Things to do for parents

Things to do for parents

There are many activities you will be able to partake with your child, such as playing on the gaming machines or enjoying the thrill of the water rides. You can also leave your child in the safe hands at the park and go on the shopping spree to the First World Plaza, which is located nearby the theme park.

Food & Accommodation

Food & Accommodation

At Genting Park you have a great selection of lovely cafes and restaurants. There are many dining experiences to be enjoyed with diverse cuisine selections, from Oriental, International to and local favourite places.

As Genting Park has many areas, we recommend staying overnight to make sure you have explored and enjoyed every one of them. You can stay at the Awana Hotel, First World Hotel and many more that are walking distance from the park.
